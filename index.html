Travel

Chicago is home to two airports, O'Hare and Midway. If you wish to use public transportation to arrive at your hotel (or visit us!), know that the CTA Blue Line can be accessed at O'Hare and the CTA Orange Line can be accessed at Midway. For more information on public transit, visit the Chicago Transit Authority website by clicking here.
